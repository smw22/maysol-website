Caja Blanca

Are you looking for a place to spend your holidays in complete privacy and surrounded by luxury and nature? This is your home. This villa is located in a residential area of Mijas Pueblo, completely independent and in a cul-de-sac. Only 15 minutes drive from Mijas Pueblo and Fuengirola, this villa is undoubtedly ideal to unwind, take a dip in the huge swimming pool, sunbathe and relax in the beautifully decorated bedrooms. It consists of a main house and a guest annexe with kitchen.
