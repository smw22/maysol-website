ROSA VILLA

Rosa Villa

Are you looking for a place to spend your holidays in front of the sea and with direct access to the beach? Then this is the place for you. This villa is literally at the foot of the Chaparral beach, a stone's throw from La Cala de Mijas and Fuengirola. The property is located in a cul-de-sac with its own parking inside the property and completely reformed. This villa is perfect to relax and enjoy the beach at all hours. You can enjoy the sea by accessing the beach from the villa or you can take a dip in the private pool that the villa has. Chaparral beach is very popular among windsurfers and surfers. There are also several restaurants within walking distance from the villa.
