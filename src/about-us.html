BEHIND THE SCENES

Real estate agent on the Costa del Sol, qualified translator and interpreter in English and Danish and entrepreneur, great mediator and facilitator in projects indirectly related to the purchase, rental and after-sales of a property.

I have worked in kitchen design, interior design, helping to choose and accompany when decorating a home. I have also advised many Scandinavian and British families on settling in Spain, from tax and legal issues to family reunification, schooling and health registration.

I like my homes to be well cared for and welcoming to all my guests. I take great care to ensure that my guest's experience is as satisfactory as possible. That's why I offer alternative plans both inside and outside the house. Whether you are looking for activities for a hen party, water or mountain sports in nature, a magnificent dinner prepared by a chef in your home, or a wedding celebration, you won't be short of ideas.
